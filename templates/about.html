About NETFLIX Analytics

Predicting market trends requires more than historical data. It requires intelligent analysis. NETFLIX Analytics combines machine learning, advanced technical indicators, and data-driven analytics to provide accurate, transparent, and easy-to-understand stock market forecasts for investors, analysts, and financial enthusiasts.

Overview

Stock prices fluctuate every day due to several market factors. Predicting future prices accurately is a challenging task because of market volatility and uncertainty. NETFLIX Analytics uses historical Netflix stock market data, performs extensive feature engineering and trains teh data to predict the next day's closing price.

The application integrates Machine Learning with Flask to provide a user friendly web interface where users can view historical prices, prediction results, performance metrics and future forecasts.

Disclaimer

NETFLIX Analytics provides predictive analytics based on historical market data and machine learning models.

Predictions are intended to assist research and educational analysis only and should not be considered financial, investment, or trading advice. Financial markets are influenced by numerous external factors, and future performance cannot be guaranteed.

Users should perform independent research and consult qualified financial professionals before making investment decisions.
